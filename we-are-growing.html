Now Serving the Commonwealth of Virginia

Since 2012 Southern Mutual Church has been growing both financially and operationally. In 2015, the company expanded its footprint and began writing in Kentucky, which was more recently followed by Virginia in 2018. The company continues to experience strong policyholder growth now insuring more than 9,400 churches and maintaining a 98% renewal retention. During the same time period, strong agency appointments, sound underwriting and loss control performance, superior claims handling and management’s execution of business strategies has led to the company growing its Policyholder Surplus by more than 125%. Founded on the principles of “Savings, Service and Security” over 90 years ago, Southern Mutual Church is dedicated to delivering upon these values to its faithful member policyholders.
